Cook With Comali

Cooku with Comali, also known by the acronym CWC, is a Tamil-language comedy-based cooking competition show aired on Star Vijay and streams on Disney+Hotstar where the contestants are paired each week with comedians (comalis) who are amateur cooks.[1] It premiered on 16 November 2019. The contestants along with their comalis are challenged every week and judged by Chef Damodharan and Chef Venkatesh Bhat

Bharathi kannamma

Bharathi Kannamma is a 2019 Indian Tamil language social dramatic television series aired on Star Vijay and streams on Disney+Hotstar. It premiered on February 25, 2019, and directed by Praveen Bennett. It stars Roshni Haripriyan and Arun Prasad.[1] The series is a remake of the Malayalam television series Karuthamuthu.[2] later in 2021, Roshni was replaced by a newcomer actress Vinusha Devi
